🌱 The Seedling

Sprout has grown into a seedling! Roots reach down, a shoot reaches up — but who's feeding Sprout while it grows?

Sprout says

"Look at me now! I've got roots below and a shoot above. But I still don't have my true leaves yet — so who's giving me the energy to grow?"

📖 Who feeds a seedling? The seed leaf is doing the feeding! Since Sprout doesn't have true leaves yet, it can't make its own food. The seed leaf's stored food keeps Sprout growing — until the true leaves are ready to take over.
